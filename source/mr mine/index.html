Update v0.46 - 7/28/25

  • New Superminers: Scrap Miners have been added, who use Relic Scrap in exchange for useful buffs.
  • New Relics: Boulder breaking, damage reduction and isotope decayment.
  • New Quests: Progression and collection quests with extra rewards.
  • Scrap Trader Revamped: More common and with new trades available.
  • More Stats: Caves fully completed, scrap spent, drill parts crafted and other stats.
  • QoL & Bug Fixes: Drone fixes, Super miner fixes, more translations, and adjusted various inconsistencies across the game.
Update v0.45 - 11/20/24

  • Battles Revamped: Now turn-based with multi-monster fights, equips from Battle Chests, and new consumables.
  • Gem Forge Overhaul: Automate gem crafting and use gems to boost battle stats.
  • Titan Extended: Final depth of 750km, trading post added, and drill balance improvements.
  • Scientists Update: New scrollable UI, relic scrap system, and excavation rewards for resurrected scientists.
  • New Cave Consumables: Fleet drones for half fuel cost and full cave reveal.
  • QoL & Bug Fixes: Improved shop organization, glowing mineral deposits, new shortcuts, and performance upgrades.

  • QoL Changes
