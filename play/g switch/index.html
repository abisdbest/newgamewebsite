G-Switch: Gravity-Defying Endless Runner

"G-Switch" is a captivating and unique endless runner that defies the laws of gravity, challenging players to navigate through intricate levels with precision and timing. Created by Vasco Freitas and published by Serius Games, this flash game has been a favorite among players since its release in 2010.

Controls:
The controls for "G-Switch" are designed for simplicity and responsiveness. Players make the character switch gravity by pressing the spacebar on the keyboard or clicking the mouse. This gravity-defying mechanic is the key to overcoming obstacles and navigating through the ever-changing environments.

The game's main mechanic is the ability to switch gravity at will, allowing the character to run on both the floor and ceiling. The objective is to survive as long as possible, avoiding obstacles and gaps by timing gravity switches with precision. The fast-paced nature of the game adds to the challenge, keeping players on the edge of their seats.

"G-Switch" can be enjoyed solo or with friends, as it features multiplayer modes where up to 8 players can compete simultaneously. The dynamic and visually engaging levels, coupled with the gravity-switching twist, create an exhilarating and replayable gaming experience.

Whether you're a fan of endless runners or enjoy defying gravity in a fast-paced environment, "G-Switch" offers a thrilling and gravity-bending adventure. Challenge yourself, test your reflexes, and embark on an endless running journey that defies the conventions of traditional platformers.
