Raft Wars
Developer:
Raft Wars is developed by Martijn Kunst.

Raft Wars: Defend Your Treasure, Sink Your Enemies!

Get ready for an epic battle on the high seas in Raft Wars, the addictive and action-packed game developed by Martijn Kunst. Join Simon and his brother as they defend their treasure against a horde of enemies using nothing but their trusty raft and a stash of water balloons.

Gameplay:
In Raft Wars, players take on the role of Simon and his brother as they fend off waves of enemies attempting to steal their treasure. Using the mouse or touchscreen controls, players aim and launch water balloons at their adversaries, strategically adjusting the angle and power to hit their targets. With each successful hit, enemies take damage and eventually sink, but beware—enemy projectiles can also damage the raft and reduce your health.

Features:
Raft Wars offers a range of features to enhance the gameplay experience. From challenging levels set in various environments to a variety of enemies with unique abilities and behaviors, the game keeps players engaged and entertained as they progress through the adventure. Additionally, players can earn coins and upgrade their raft and weapons to improve their chances of victory against increasingly difficult foes.

Endless Fun:
Raft Wars is not just about defending your treasure—it's about having fun and enjoying the thrill of the battle. With its charming graphics, humorous dialogue, and addictive gameplay, Raft Wars offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're playing solo or challenging a friend in multiplayer mode, Raft Wars promises non-stop excitement and laughter from start to finish.

Are you ready to embark on an unforgettable adventure with Simon and his brother in Raft Wars? Gather your water balloons, man the raft, and prepare to defend your treasure against all odds in this epic battle for supremacy on the high seas!
