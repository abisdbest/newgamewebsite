Ice Dodo: A Space Odyssey Created by Dododo73 and Onionfist Studio
Developer:
Ice Dodo and other Onionfist games were created by Jason Yeon, also known as Seojoon or Dododo73. He embarked on this creative journey when he was just 13 years old.

Year of Creation:
Ice Dodo was crafted by Dododo73 around the time of his teenage years, showcasing early talent and ingenuity.

Ice Dodo: An Icy Adventure Across the Cosmos

Ice Dodo, a charming creation from the mind of Dododo73 and Onionfist Studio, takes players on an enchanting journey through the cosmos. Born out of Dododo73's imaginative spirit at the age of 13, the game introduces players to an ice cube embarking on a space odyssey.

Development and Naming:
Dododo73, also known as Jason Yeon, developed Ice Dodo with inspiration drawn from his Minecraft username and a captivating concept of an ice cube navigating the vastness of space. The game's title, "Ice Dodo," reflects Dododo73's Minecraft alias and the central theme of an icy protagonist venturing through the cosmic unknown.

Onionfist Studio Collaboration:
Onionfist Studio, the creative hub behind Ice Dodo and several other games, is a collaborative effort that extends beyond Dododo73. The team comprises Mapmakers, Pixel Artists, and Music Creators, contributing their skills to enhance the overall gaming experience.

List of Mapmakers:
Dododo73, Carrots, skilledandkilled, Xdoomination, Furby, Rocky707, and a multitude of talented individuals form the Mapmaking team, crafting the immersive and diverse landscapes within Onionfist games.

List of Pixel Artists:
ItsYaBoiPoppin, Furby, Daniel, and a host of other Pixel Artists contribute their artistic talents, shaping the visual aesthetics of Onionfist games, including the delightful Ice Dodo.

List of Music Creators:
EnV, ColBreakz, The Insolence Watches You, and others are the creative minds behind the music, providing captivating soundscapes that enhance the gaming experience in Ice Dodo and beyond.

Partnerships:
Onionfist has established partnerships with Doppler and Kristian, broadening the collaborative scope and contributing to the success of their gaming endeavors.

Ice Dodo stands as a testament to the creative prowess of Dododo73 and the collaborative spirit of Onionfist Studio. With its unique concept, engaging gameplay, and a dedicated team of contributors, Ice Dodo invites players to embark on a whimsical and icy adventure through the cosmic wonders crafted by a passionate and talented community.
